Unregistered visitor
An unregistered visitor can see blogs, existing site content and selected articles from the current magazine
Registered visitor
A registered visitor can see everything an unregistered visitor can see, but can also see one or two extra articles.
Starter member
A starter member pays £1 a month for extra web access. They get everything a registered member gets and in addition access to some (all?) magazine content (up to 20 pieces/ about half of total), archive access and shop discounts and offers.
Full member — print
A full print member pays £40 a year. She gets everything a starter member gets and in addition a subscription to the print magazine and unlimited access to all archive and magazine content on the website.
Full member — digital
A full digital member pays £40 a year. She gets everything a starter member gets and in addition a subscription to the app and exact editions versions of the magazine and unlimited access to all archive and magazine content on the website.
Premium member
A premium member pays £50 or more a year. She gets everythig that a full digital member and a full print member gets (a subscription to the print, app and exact editions versions of the magazine and unlimited access to all archive and magazine content on the website). In addition she gets Free e-books; bigger discounts; early-bird digital access to articles, letters
